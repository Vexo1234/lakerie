OUR STORY

Founded in late 2024, Lake Erie Shadow Hunters is a unique father-and-son paranormal investigation team based in Ashtabula, Ohio. Christian and his 13-year-old son Caden (who many say is the youngest investigator they know) explore haunted locations throughout Ohio and maybe even other states 👀.

What began as a shared interest in the paranormal has grown into a special bonding experience that strengthens our relationship while documenting the paranormal. We're passionate about sharing our experiences with the world.

Our approach combines traditional paranormal research methods with the fresh perspective of a new generation, creating a unique dynamic that helps us connect with both spirits and our growing audience.
